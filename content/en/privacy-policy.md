### Tracking Technologies & Cookies
We use Cookies and other similar tracking technologies to keep tabs on activity on our Service and stash away certain info. The tracking technologies we use include beacons, tags, and scripts. They're all about gathering and tracking info, plus improving and analyzing our Service. The tech we use might include:

- **Cookies or Browser Cookies**: A Cookie is a tiny file dropped onto your Device. You can tell your browser to give all Cookies the boot or to give you a heads-up when one's being sent. But, if you don't play nice with Cookies, you might miss out on some cool parts of our Service. Unless you've tweaked your browser settings to say "no thanks" to Cookies, our Service might just use 'em.
- **Web Beacons**: Some parts of our Service and our emails might have tiny electronic files called Web Beacons (also known as clear gifs, pixel tags, and single-pixel gifs). These little guys let the Company (for example) count users who have bopped around those pages or opened an email. They also help with other website stats, like figuring out how popular a certain section is and checking system and server integrity. Pretty neat, huh? ✨

Cookies can be "Persistent" or "Session" Cookies. When you're offline, Persistent Cookies stick around on your personal computer or mobile device. Session Cookies, on the other hand, vanish the moment you close your web browser. Poof!

We use both Session and Persistent Cookies for the purposes listed below. We've got our reasons!

- **Necessary / Essential Cookies**
  - **Type**: Session Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are super important for giving you the services available through the Website and letting you use some of its cool features. They help us verify users and prevent any funky, fraudulent use of user accounts. Without these Cookies, the services you've asked for simply can't happen, and we only use these Cookies to make sure you get those services. Can't live without 'em! 💖

- **Cookies Policy / Notice Acceptance Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are used to check if users have given us the thumbs-up to use Cookies on the Website. It's all about your choice! 👍

- **Functional Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are neat because they help us remember the choices you make when you're cruising around the Website. Think remembering your login deets or language preferences. The goal of these Cookies? To give you a super personalized experience, so you don't have to keep re-entering your preferences every single time you visit. Sweet! ✨

For more deets about the Cookies we use and your choices regarding 'em, check out our Cookies Policy or the Cookies section of our Privacy Policy.
